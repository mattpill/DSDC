Javeon

Since signing with PMR records in 2013, Javeon has released several singles and collaborated with artists such as Julio Bashmore, Cyril Hahn and Le Youth. In recent months, and with his signing to Seventy Hertz Publishing, he’s developed a deeper, darker and more personal sound.

We were approached to create the artwork for Javeon’s new release, which would convey this darker side to his sound. We avoided the use of portrait photography and instead focused on conveying some of the darker emotions explored on the EP with the use of a limited colour palette and abstract photography.
